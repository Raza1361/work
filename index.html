About Farah

Healing Society Through Words

Farah Misbah is a renowned poet, writer, motivational speaker, and match maker of the present era. She plays a significant role in healing our society through her profound writings and inspirational speeches.

As the author of the spectacular book "Dastan e Ishq o Safar," Farah has touched countless hearts and minds with her eloquent expression of human emotions and experiences.

Her literary contributions have been recognized at the national level, with her articles being included in secondary school textbooks as part of Pakistan's educational action plan.

Farah Misbah has had an illustrious career as a presenter and anchor from 2003 to 2024. She began her journey with Radio Pakistan, where she showcased her exceptional communication skills and ability to engage with diverse audiences. Over the years, she became a prominent figure in the industry, joining ARY QTV as a presenter, where she focused on religious and spiritual programs, earning widespread appreciation for her insightful content and engaging style. She further expanded her repertoire by working as an anchor on ARY City, demonstrating her versatility in covering a variety of topics and connecting with urban audiences. Farah's two-decade-long career reflects her dedication, professionalism, and the trust she has earned from esteemed platforms and her audience alike. She Is Working As A Writer And Motivational Speaker Right Now.
