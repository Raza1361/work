Discover the World of Farah Misbah

Farah Misbah is the poet, Writer, motivational speaker, and Match Maker of the present era. She is playing a big role in healing this shattered society through her writings and speeches. She is also the author of the spectacular book "Dastan-e-Ishq-o-Safar." In Pakistan's educational action plan, her articles have been included in the secondary book curriculum.
